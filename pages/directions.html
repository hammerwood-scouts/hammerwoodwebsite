How to Find Us

Hammerwood is located off the A264, between East Grinstead and Holtye. Follow the signs for Hammerwood Park into Hammerwood hamlet. Travel down the wiggly road and when you pass the barns on the left you need to take a right turn, at Bower House, before the sign marked "private road". Travel down the track past the fields until you reach the campsite. On entering the campsite, you must immediately turn right into the car park. (Note: this track is not suitable for anything larger than an ordinary width minibus.)
