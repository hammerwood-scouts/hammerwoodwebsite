Logo Competition -Register Below








Guidance: don't forget to do some research before creating your logo. Try to encorporate the essense of Hammerwood Scout Campsite in your design. Consider researching the history, the area, and logos for similar sites as part of your preparation. You could create a mood board to help lay out your ideas.

Terms and Conditions

In the terms stated below, 'Entrant' refers to the person submitting their design, or their legal guardian, should the entrant be under 18 years of age. 'Committee' refers to the Management Committee of Hammerwood Scout Campsite, or any of its individual members.


Entry Requirements:

  1. Entrants must be a member of, or related to a member of, either 9th LN, 12th LN, 17th LN, 21st LN, 25th LN, or 28th LN to enter.
  2. There is no age limit on applications - leaders and parents can submit designs too!
  3. Entrants must register using the form above, before or after submitting an entry and before the competition deadline: 9th August 2020.
  4. Entrants can submit more than one entry. Only one registration form is required per person.

Registration and Submission:

  1. If your logo is designed on paper, please submit a high quality photo. If your design is created using software, please send the original file and a .jpeg or .png version.
  2. Entrants must register using the form above, before the competition deadline: 9th August 2020 (midnight).
  3. To submit a design, please email your image (and your name and group) to hammerwoodscouts@gmail.com before the submission deadline: 9th August 2020 (midnight). Please ensure that you have also completed a registration form.
  4. Entrants can submit more than one entry. Only one registration form is required per person.
  5. We take no responsibility for entries not received for whatever reason.
  6. By submitting an entry, entrants confirm that any drawings submitted are not defamatory, unlawful, discriminatory, or malicious, do not infringe third-party rights and are not subject to conflicting agreements in place that restrict their use.
  7. By submitting an entry, entrants agree that any personal information provided with the entry may be held and used by the Committee to administer the competition and/or to fulfil, deliver or arrange the prize.

The Design:

  1. Please aim to create a logo no smaller than 10cm x 10cm / 10cm diameter.
  2. The design can, but does not have to, include the text "Hammerwood Scout Campsite".
  3. The design can be black and white, or include colour.

The Winner:

  1. The winning design will be confirmed at Hammerwood Scout Campsite AGM in September 2020.
  2. The winning entry will be professionally recreated and may differ from the original artwork submitted.
  3. The Committee's desisions are final.
  4. The winning entrant will be contacted within 7 days of the decision being made, to arrange delivery of a £25 voucher of the entrant's (or parent's) choice. The voucher must be available for purchase online and will be emailed to the winner. If no response is received from the winner within 30 days of contact, the prize amount will be donated to charity.

Use of Designs:

  1. By submitting an entry, entrants agree that the Committee may, but is not required to, make any design submitted, available on its websites, social media pages and any other media, whether now known or invented in the future, and in connection with any publicity of the competition.
  2. Entrants grant the Committee a non-exclusive, worldwide, irrevocable license, for the full period of any intellectual property rights in any drawing, to use, display, publish, transmit, copy, edit, alter, store, reformat and sub-license the drawing for such purposes.
